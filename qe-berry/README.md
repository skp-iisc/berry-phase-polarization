# Calculations on Quantum ESPRESSO

Calculations are done for 3 different types of pseudopotentials. The lattice optimization results is shown in `alat_en/` folder.

Final results:

| Quantities | PZ PP | ONCV PBE PP | ONCV PZ PP | Results from ref. | Experiments (ref.) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| $a$ | 10.58 | 10.58 | 10.58 | 10.59 | 10.69 |
| $(Z^*)_{11}$ | 2.165 | 2.336 | 2.360 | 2.14 | 2.18 |
| $(\epsilon_{\infty})_{11}$ | 8.234 | 8.882 | 8.953 | 9.6 | 8.2 |
| $(\epsilon_{stat})_{11}$ | 9.906 | 10.832 | 10.937 | 11.5 | 10.1 |

The ref. is *Ivo Souza, Jorge Iniguez, and David Vanderbilt; PRL 89, 117602 (2002)*.
